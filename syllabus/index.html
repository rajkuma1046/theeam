SSC Syllabus 2025–26

Exam-wise, tier-wise, subject-wise detailed syllabus for all SSC exams. Based on official SSC notifications.

📊 SSC CGL — Exam Pattern

Tier I100 Questions / 200 Marks / 60 min — Screening Test (CBT)
Tier IIPaper I: 3 modules / 2 hrs 30 min — Main Exam (CBT)
Negative MarkingTier I: 0.50 per wrong answer  |  Tier II: 1 mark per wrong answer

📚 SSC CGL — Detailed Syllabus

🧠 General Intelligence & Reasoning
Analogies, Similarities & Differences, Space Visualization, Spatial Orientation, Problem Solving, Analysis, Judgment, Decision Making, Visual Memory, Discrimination, Observation, Relationship Concepts, Arithmetical Reasoning, Verbal & Figure Classification, Arithmetical Number Series, Non-Verbal Series, Coding & Decoding, Statement & Conclusion, Syllogistic Reasoning, Blood Relations, Direction & Distance, Input-Output, Seating Arrangement, Puzzles
📰 General Awareness
Indian History (Ancient, Medieval, Modern), Indian Polity & Constitution, Indian Economy, Geography (India & World), Environmental Science, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer Technology, Current Events, Sports, Awards & Honours, Books & Authors, Important Dates, Scientific Research
🔢 Quantitative Aptitude
Number Systems, Computation of Whole Numbers, Decimals & Fractions, Relationship between Numbers, Fundamental Arithmetic Operations, Percentage, Ratio & Proportion, Square Roots, Averages, Interest (Simple & Compound), Profit & Loss, Discount, Partnership, Mixture & Alligation, Time & Distance, Work & Time, Basic Algebraic Identities, Linear Equations, Graphs, Triangle & Circles, Mensuration, Trigonometry, Height & Distance, Data Interpretation
📝 English Language
Spot the Error, Fill in the Blanks, Synonyms/Homonyms, Antonyms, Spellings/Detecting Mis-spelt Words, Idioms & Phrases, One-Word Substitution, Improvement of Sentences, Active/Passive Voice, Direct/Indirect Speech, Para Jumbles, Cloze Passage, Reading Comprehension, Sentence Completion

📊 SSC CHSL — Exam Pattern

Tier I (CBT)100 Questions / 200 Marks / 60 min — Screening
Tier II Session IModule I + II (Objective) + Module III (Computer) — 2 hrs 15 min
Tier II Session IISkill/Typing Test — Qualifying
Negative MarkingTier I: 0.50  |  Tier II: 1 mark per wrong answer

📚 SSC CHSL — Syllabus

SSC CHSL Tier I syllabus is similar to SSC CGL Tier I — General Intelligence, Quantitative Aptitude, English, General Awareness (25 Qs each).

Tier II Key Additions:

  • Mathematical Abilities: Number System, Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry, Statistics, Data Interpretation
  • English Language & Comprehension: Advanced Grammar, Reading Comprehension, Vocabulary
  • Computer Knowledge (Qualifying): MS Office, Internet, Basics of Computer Operations
  • Typing Test (PA/SA/LDC): English 35 wpm / Hindi 30 wpm

📊 SSC GD — Exam Pattern

CBT80 Questions / 160 Marks / 60 min
PET/PSTPhysical Efficiency Test & Physical Standard Test — Qualifying
MedicalMedical Examination
Negative Marking0.50 per wrong answer

📚 SSC GD — Syllabus

🧠 General Intelligence & Reasoning (20 Qs)
Analogies, Similarities, Differences, Spatial Visualization, Spatial Orientation, Visual Memory, Discrimination, Observation, Arithmetic Reasoning, Verbal & Figure Classification, Number Series, Coding-Decoding
📰 General Knowledge & Awareness (20 Qs)
India's History, Culture, Geography, Economic Scene, General Polity, Indian Constitution, Scientific Research, Current Events, Sports
🔢 Elementary Mathematics (20 Qs)
Number Systems, HCF & LCM, Decimals & Fractions, Percentage, Ratio & Proportion, Averages, Profit & Loss, Simple Interest, Time & Work, Time & Distance, Mensuration
📝 English / Hindi (20 Qs)
Basics of English Grammar, Reading Comprehension, Vocabulary, Synonyms, Antonyms, Fill in the Blanks (OR equivalent Hindi language questions)

📊 SSC MTS — Exam Pattern

Session I (CBT)Numerical & Mathematical + Reasoning — 40 Questions / 45 min
Session II (CBT)General Awareness + English — 50 Questions / 45 min
Negative MarkingNo negative marking

📚 SSC MTS — Syllabus

  • Numerical & Mathematical Ability: Number System, Decimals, Fractions, LCM, HCF, Ratio & Proportion, Percentage, Simple & Compound Interest, Profit & Loss, Discount, Time & Work, Time & Distance, Tables & Graphs, Mensuration
  • Reasoning & Problem Solving: Analogies, Similarities & Differences, Spatial Visualization, Problem Solving, Analysis, Judgment, Decision Making, Arithmetic Reasoning, Non-Verbal Series, Coding-Decoding
  • General Awareness: India & its Neighbours, History, Culture, Geography, Economy, Polity, Constitution, Sports, Awards, Current Events, Scientific Research
  • English Language: Basics of English language, Vocabulary, Grammar, Sentence Structure, Spot the Error, Fill in the Blanks, Synonyms, Antonyms, One Word Substitution

📊 SSC CPO — Exam Pattern

Paper I (CBT)200 Questions / 200 Marks / 2 hrs
PET/PSTPhysical Test — Qualifying
Paper II (CBT)200 Questions / 200 Marks / 2 hrs — English Language
MedicalMedical Examination
Negative Marking0.25 per wrong answer

📚 SSC CPO — Syllabus (Paper I)

  • General Intelligence & Reasoning (50 Qs): Same as CGL — Analogies, series, coding, seating, blood relations, syllogism, etc.
  • General Knowledge & Awareness (50 Qs): History, polity, economics, science, sports, current affairs
  • Quantitative Aptitude (50 Qs): Arithmetic, algebra, geometry, trigonometry, data interpretation
  • English Comprehension (50 Qs): Reading comprehension, cloze test, idioms, error spotting, sentence improvement

📊 SSC JE — Exam Pattern

Paper I200 Questions / 200 Marks / 2 hrs — General + Technical
Paper II150 Questions / 300 Marks / 2 hrs — Advanced Technical
Negative MarkingPaper I: 0.25 per wrong answer  |  Paper II: 1 mark per wrong answer

📚 SSC JE — Technical Syllabus Branches

Civil Engineering Topics:

  • Building Materials, Estimating & Costing, Surveying, Soil Mechanics, Hydraulics, Irrigation, Transportation Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Design of Concrete & Steel Structures

Electrical Engineering Topics:

  • Basic Electrical Engineering, Electrical Machines, Power Systems, Measurements, Electronics & Communication

Mechanical Engineering Topics:

  • Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics & Hydraulic Machinery, Theory of Machines, Machine Design, Manufacturing Science, Production Engineering, Industrial Engineering
