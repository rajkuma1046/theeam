🔧 SSC Exam — Junior Engineer

SSC JE 2025–26

SSC Junior Engineer Examination — for Group B (Non-Gazetted) posts in Civil, Electrical, Mechanical & Quantity Surveying engineering across Central Government departments.

📄 Official Notification 📚 Syllabus ✅ Eligibility 🔴 Latest Updates

Conducting BodyStaff Selection Commission (SSC)

Post LevelGroup B, Non-Gazetted
NotificationExpected Jul–Aug 2025
Selection ProcessPaper I (CBT) → Paper II (Descriptive/CBT)

✅ Eligibility Criteria

🎓
Educational Qualification Degree or Diploma in Civil / Electrical / Mechanical Engineering from a recognized University/Institution. Candidate must apply for the branch matching their qualification.
Civil Electrical Mechanical QS & Contracts
🎂
Age Limit18–32 years. Relaxation: SC/ST (5 years), OBC (3 years), PwD (10 years), Govt. servants (5 years), J&K domicile (5 years).
🇮🇳
NationalityIndian citizen. Other eligible persons as specified in the official notification.

📚 Exam Pattern & Syllabus

📋 Paper I — Objective (200 Marks)

Duration: 2 hours  |  Questions: 200  |  Negative Marking: 0.25 per wrong answer


  • Part A — General Intelligence & Reasoning: 50 Questions / 50 Marks
  • Part B — General Awareness: 50 Questions / 50 Marks
  • Part C — Technical (Civil/Electrical/Mechanical/QS&C): 100 Questions / 100 Marks
📋 Paper II — Technical (300 Marks)

Duration: 2 hours  |  Mode: CBT (Objective)  |  Questions: 150


  • Civil/Structural: Building Materials, Estimating, Surveying, Soil Mechanics, Design of Steel/Concrete Structures, etc.
  • Electrical: Basic Electrical Engineering, Electrical Machines, Power Systems, Measurements, Electronics, etc.
  • Mechanical: Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Theory of Machines, Machine Design, Production Engineering, etc.
