💡 Top Study Tips for SSC Exams

⏰ Time Management
Allocate fixed time slots for each subject daily. Spend more time on weak areas but don't neglect strong ones.
📝 Practice Daily
Solve at least 50 questions every day from mixed topics. Quality of practice matters more than quantity.
📰 Current Affairs
Read one newspaper daily or follow our monthly digest. Focus on national & international importance for GK.
🎯 Mock Tests
Take at least 2 full-length mock tests per week. Analyse mistakes carefully and avoid repeating them.
