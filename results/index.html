📌 How to Check SSC Result

  1. Go to ssc.gov.in (official SSC website).
  2. Click on the "Result" section from the top menu or the latest notices section on the homepage.
  3. Select the relevant exam (CGL, CHSL, GD, MTS, etc.) and year.
  4. The result PDF will open. Search for your Roll Number using Ctrl+F.
  5. If selected, candidates are sent an email/SMS as well.
