About SSCExamPrep

Independent · Non-governmental · Education only

⚠️ Independence Notice: SSCExamPrep is a privately run educational website. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Staff Selection Commission (SSC) or the Government of India in any way.

🎯 Our Mission

SSCExamPrep was built with one purpose: to give every SSC aspirant in India a clean, fast, and trustworthy place to track exam updates — without drowning in ads, fake news, or clickbait headlines.

We believe that a government job aspirant working hard every day deserves accurate information delivered honestly. When you check our site for a result or admit card, you should never have to question whether what you're reading is real.

Everything we publish comes directly from ssc.gov.in — the official SSC website — and linked to the original source so you can verify it yourself.

📋 What We Cover

We track all major Staff Selection Commission examinations conducted by SSC (India):

  • SSC CGL — Combined Graduate Level (Group B & C posts)
  • SSC CHSL — Combined Higher Secondary Level (LDC, DEO, PA/SA posts)
  • SSC GD — General Duty Constable (CISF, BSF, CRPF, ITBP, SSB, Assam Rifles)
  • SSC MTS — Multi-Tasking Staff & Havaldar (CBIC & CBN)
  • SSC CPO — Central Police Organisation (SI in Delhi Police & CAPFs)
  • SSC JE — Junior Engineer (Civil, Electrical, Mechanical)
  • SSC Stenographer — Grade C & D
  • SSC CHSL, SSC Phase examinations, and other periodic notifications

For each exam, we track: official notifications, application open/close dates, admit cards, exam dates, answer keys, cut-off marks, and final results.

🔍 How We Source Information

We follow a strict sourcing policy. Every update published on this site goes through the following process:

  • Primary source: Only ssc.gov.in and official SSC regional portals are accepted as primary sources for notifications, results, and admit cards.
  • No rumours or leaks: We do not publish expected dates, rumoured vacancies, or unconfirmed information without clearly labelling it as "Expected" or "Unofficial".
  • Direct links: Every update on our Latest Updates page links directly to the official SSC portal for verification.
  • Corrections: If we publish an error, we correct it immediately and note the correction. See our Editorial Policy.

📱 Why We're Different

  • Mobile-first design — Built for the 360px Android screens most aspirants use
  • No clickbait headlines — We state facts, not sensationalism
  • Official source transparency — Every update is linked back to ssc.gov.in
  • Fast loading — No heavy scripts, no unnecessary plugins
