SSC CPO 2025–26

SSC CPO Examination — for Sub-Inspector in Delhi Police, CAPFs (BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, SSB) and Assistant Sub-Inspector in CISF. A gateway to Police service.

📄 Official Notification 📚 Syllabus ✅ Eligibility 🔴 Latest Updates

📊 SSC CPO 2025 — At a Glance

Conducting BodyStaff Selection Commission (SSC)
PostsSI in Delhi Police, CAPFs; ASI in CISF
NotificationExpected Aug–Sep 2025
Application ModeOnline — ssc.gov.in
Selection ProcessPaper I (CBT) → PET/PST → Paper II (CBT) → Medical
Pay Scale (SI)Pay Level 6 (₹35,400–1,12,400)
Pay Scale (ASI-CISF)Pay Level 5 (₹29,200–92,300)
Answer KeyPaper-I Answer Key: Jun 2025

✅ Eligibility Criteria

🎓
Educational QualificationBachelor's Degree from a recognized University or equivalent. No specific stream required unless mentioned otherwise.
🎂
Age Limit20–25 years for SI posts. Relaxation: SC/ST (5 years), OBC (3 years), Govt. servant (5 years), Ex-Servicemen as applicable.
📏
Physical StandardsMale: Height 170 cm, Chest 80–85 cm; Female: Height 157 cm. Relaxation for candidates from hill regions and specified communities.
👁️
Eye SightNear Vision: N6 (better eye) / N9 (worse eye). Distant Vision: 6/6 (better eye) / 6/9 (worse eye) without glasses.

📚 Exam Pattern & Syllabus

📋 Paper I — CBT (200 Marks)

Duration: 2 hours  |  Total Questions: 200  |  Negative Marking: 0.25 per wrong answer


  • General Intelligence & Reasoning — 50 Questions / 50 Marks
  • General Knowledge & Awareness — 50 Questions / 50 Marks
  • Quantitative Aptitude — 50 Questions / 50 Marks
  • English Comprehension — 50 Questions / 50 Marks
📋 Paper II — English (200 Marks)

Duration: 2 hours  |  Questions: 200  |  Negative Marking: 0.25 per wrong answer


  • Reading Comprehension, Cloze Test, Error Spotting
  • Fill in the Blanks, Synonyms & Antonyms, Idioms & Phrases
  • One-word Substitution, Sentence Improvement, Para Jumbles
  • Active/Passive Voice, Direct/Indirect Speech
🏃 Physical Efficiency Test (PET)

PET is qualifying in nature. Candidates must pass to proceed.


Male — Race100 m in 16 seconds & 1.6 km in 6.5 minutes
Male — Long Jump3.65 m (3 chances)
Male — High Jump1.2 m (3 chances)
Female — Race100 m in 18 seconds & 800 m in 4 minutes
Female — Long Jump2.7 m (3 chances)
Female — High Jump0.9 m (3 chances)
