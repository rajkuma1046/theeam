Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2025

Summary (Plain Language): We collect minimal data — only what's needed to operate the site (analytics) and respond to your messages. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not store sensitive personal information.

1. Who We Are

SSCExamPrep ("we", "us", "our") operates the website sscexamprep.online — an independent educational platform for SSC exam information in India. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our site or contact us.

For questions about this policy, contact us at rajkuma1046@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

2a. Information You Provide Voluntarily

When you use our Contact & Feedback form, you may provide:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message content and query type

This information is used solely to respond to your query. We do not add you to marketing lists without explicit consent.

2b. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit sscexamprep.online, our hosting infrastructure (GitHub Pages) and analytics tools may automatically collect:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, time and date of visit
  • Device data: Device type (mobile/desktop), screen resolution, language settings
  • Cookies: Small text files placed on your device (see Section 4)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your messages — When you submit a feedback form or email us
  • To improve the site — Analytics data tells us which pages are useful and which load slowly
  • To diagnose technical issues — Error logs help fix broken links and display problems
  • To understand our audience — Anonymous aggregate data (e.g. "80% of users are on mobile") guides site design decisions

We do not use your data for advertising profiling, selling to third parties, spam campaigns, or any automated decision-making that affects you.

4. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site functionality (e.g. remembering your consent). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): Help us understand site usage in aggregate. These are anonymous and do not identify you personally. You can opt out via Google's opt-out browser add-on.

We do not use advertising, tracking, or social media cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

  • GitHub Pages — Our website is hosted by GitHub (Microsoft). GitHub may log access data per their own privacy policy: github.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics — We use Google Analytics to measure site traffic. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Formspree — If you submit our contact form, your submission may be processed by Formspree. Their privacy policy: formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
  • Google Fonts — We load fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log font requests.

We do not control how these third-party services handle data. We recommend reviewing their individual privacy policies.

6. Data Storage & Retention

Contact form submissions received via email are retained for a maximum of 12 months, after which they are permanently deleted. Analytics data is retained for 14 months (Google Analytics default) before automatic deletion.

We do not store credit card numbers, Aadhaar numbers, passwords, or any sensitive personal data. We never ask for such information.

7. Your Rights (Under Indian IT Act & DPDP Act 2023)

Under applicable Indian law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act), you have the right to:

  • Access — Request what personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your data
  • Grievance redressal — Raise a complaint about how we handle your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at rajkuma1046@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our site is intended for SSC exam aspirants who are typically 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at rajkuma1046@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact for Privacy Concerns

For any privacy-related queries, requests, or complaints:

We are committed to resolving privacy concerns fairly and promptly.
