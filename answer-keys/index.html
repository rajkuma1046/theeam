⚠️ How to Challenge an Answer Key

⏰ Challenges can be submitted only within the window provided (usually 1–3 days). Fee: ₹100 per question challenged. Fee is refunded if the challenge is accepted.
1
Log in at ssc.gov.in using your Registration Number and Date of Birth.
2
Navigate to "Candidate Login" → "Answer Key Challenge".
3
Select the Question ID you want to challenge and enter your suggested correct answer with supporting references.
4
Pay the challenge fee (₹100 per question) through online payment. Fee is non-refundable if rejected.
5
Submit before the challenge window closes. Keep the receipt/confirmation for your records.
