📌 How to Download SSC Admit Card

1
Visit the official SSC website at ssc.gov.in or your regional SSC portal (e.g., sscnr.net.in, sscsr.gov.in).
2
Click on "Admit Card" from the top navigation menu or "Latest Notices" section.
3
Select the relevant exam name and exam year from the list.
4
Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth (or Password).
5
Click "Submit" to view and download your admit card as a PDF. Take a colour printout.

🎒 What to Carry on Exam Day

🪪 Admit Card Printout
📛 Valid Photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Passport)
📸 Passport-size Photos (2 copies)
🖊️ Black/Blue Ballpoint Pen
⌚ Transparent Water Bottle
✅ Rough Sheet (if allowed)

⚠️ Electronic devices, mobile phones and smartwatches are NOT allowed inside the exam hall.
