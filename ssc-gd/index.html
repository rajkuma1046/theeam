✅ Eligibility Criteria

🎓
Educational QualificationMatriculation (Class 10th) pass from a recognized Board. No higher qualification required.
🎂
Age Limit18–23 years. Relaxation: SC/ST (5 years), OBC (3 years), Ex-Servicemen (3 years after deducting military service), PwD as per norms.
🇮🇳
NationalityIndian citizen. Gorkha and Garhwali residents with domicile of certain hill districts of Uttarakhand are also eligible for specific forces.
📏
Physical StandardsHeight: Male 170 cm / Female 157 cm (relaxed for hill regions). Chest (Male): 80 cm (unexpanded) / 85 cm (expanded). Weight: Proportionate to height & age.

🏃 Physical Efficiency Test (PET)

PET is qualifying in nature. Candidates failing PET are not considered for further stages.

📚 CBT Syllabus

📋 Computer Based Test (CBT)

Duration: 60 minutes  |  Questions: 80  |  Max Marks: 160  |  Negative Marking: 0.50 per wrong answer


  • General Intelligence & Reasoning — 20 Questions / 40 Marks — Analogies, series, coding, puzzles, spatial reasoning
  • General Knowledge & General Awareness — 20 Questions / 40 Marks — India's history, geography, polity, science, current affairs
  • Elementary Mathematics — 20 Questions / 40 Marks — Number system, HCF/LCM, percentage, ratio, time & work, mensuration
  • English / Hindi — 20 Questions / 40 Marks — Vocabulary, grammar, comprehension (medium of your choice)
